Early Years Digital Literacy

A reference guide translating abstract K-8 concepts into offline, developmentally appropriate behaviors for 3 and 4-year-olds.

Translation Core Map

Broad Topic Pre-K Theme Offline Action / Focus
Healthy Habits Transitions & Time Practicing how to stop an activity and put a device away without a meltdown.
Relationships Faces First Looking at the person talking to us instead of a screen.
Privacy & Safety Ask a Grown-Up Knowing to always ask an adult before touching a screen.
Cyberbullying Kind Words & Turns Sharing technology, taking turns, and recognizing feelings.
Identity Real Me vs Picture Understanding a photo or drawing represents a real person.
Media Literacy Real vs. Pretend Sorting real things from things in books, shows, or games.
